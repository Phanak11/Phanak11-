GIS Portfolio & Spatial Analysis Projects

Flint Water Contamination Analysis

With this map we can see there are quite a few areas with heavy cross over between the three contaminants plaguing Flint Michigan. We used buffer to see which zones would cross over and how often they overlap. The highest risk zones are the areas where all three zones overlapped, however it is important to note there are many zones where just two exist and that is dangerous as well. This map can help those trying to fix the crisis in Flint see which zones should be prioritized

Philadelphia Healthy Food Access Analysis

This map highlights 8 different subsects that would work for the placement of a new Healthy Food store. However, the best location is ObjectID:2 (highlighted) as it has the biggest area that is underserved while being near transportation of both reginal rails and high speed trains in Philadelphia. It has a square feet of: 9,569,114. Not only expressing need, but allowing space to build the store.

Pennsylvania County Voting Analysis

This map displays the 2020 Presidential Election results across Pennsylvania counties using a vote intensity classification. Counties are symbolized based on the strength of Democratic or Republican support, highlighting clear regional patterns, with stronger Republican support in central and northern areas and stronger Democratic support in urban counties such as Philadelphia and Pittsburgh.
